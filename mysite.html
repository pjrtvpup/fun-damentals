Music Writing

I studied Journalism, Gender Studies, and Philosphy at Rutgers in New Brunswick, NJ. While there I DJ'ed and managed their community radio station 90.3 The Core and wrote for The Daily Targum as well as our station's blog. I went on to contribute writing to a (now-defunct) blog called Dingus On Music, and worked for several years as a clerk at Vintage Vinyl.

I moved to Philadelphia near the end of 2016, and have since been a contributing writer to The Deli Magazine. I am now a DJ at WPRB 103.3 FM in Princeton, while working full time as a woodworker at Peg & Awl.

I also make sample-based electronic music with hardware instruments. My current project is The Candid Valley
